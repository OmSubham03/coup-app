📜 Coup Rules

Overview

Each player starts with 2 coins and 2 hidden influence cards. Last player with influence wins.

Actions (anyone can take)

  • Income — Take 1 coin
  • Foreign Aid — Take 2 coins (blockable by Duke)
  • Coup — Pay 7 coins, target loses 1 influence (mandatory at 10+)

Character Actions

  • Duke — Tax: Take 3 coins. Blocks Foreign Aid
  • Assassin — Assassinate: Pay 3 coins, target loses influence (blockable by Contessa)
  • Captain — Steal: Take 2 coins from target (blockable by Captain/Ambassador)
  • Ambassador — Exchange: Draw 2 cards, keep what you want. Blocks stealing
  • Contessa — Blocks assassination

Inquisitor Variant

  • Inquisitor — Exchange: Draw 1 card, keep what you want. Can interrogate a player to peek at a card and optionally force them to swap it. Blocks stealing

Bluffing & Challenges

You can claim any character action regardless of your cards. Any player can challenge a claim. If the challenge succeeds (bluff caught), the liar loses influence. If the challenge fails, the challenger loses influence and the claimer swaps their revealed card.

Blocking

Certain actions can be blocked by claiming a counter-character. Blocks can also be challenged.
